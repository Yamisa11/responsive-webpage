Historical Places

City Hall

City Hall is a large Edwardian building that was built in 1905 from honey-coloured oolitic limestone imported from Bath, England.

Iziko Slave Lodge

This building housed more than 9000 slaves centuries ago. It has since changed names a four times, today namely the Slave Lodge.

Castle of Good Hope
